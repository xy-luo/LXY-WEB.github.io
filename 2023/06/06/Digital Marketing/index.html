Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is the marketing of products or services using digital technologies, mainly on the Internet, but also including mobile phones, display advertising, and any other digital medium.

Digital Marketing Vehicles

1. Orgainc

Direct or bookmarked traffic.
The definition of organic marketing refers to the act of getting your customers to come to you naturally over time, rather than “artificially” via paid link or boosted posts. It includes any direct, instinct tools with the exception of paid marketing tools.

2. SEM

CPC-Cost Per Click
Actual CPC = AdRank of Ad below you / your quality score + 0.01

Impression: 广告被浏览数
Clicks：广告被点击数
Click-Through Rate(%) = Clicks / Impression （看到的人里有多少会点击广告）

Conversions: 通过广告转化为实际用户
Conversion Rate: Conversions / Clicks

Cost per Click：每次点击花费多少钱
Total Cost：Cost per Click * Clicks
Cost per Acquisition: total Cost / total Conversions

Match type

  1. Exact Match
  2. Broad Match
  3. Phrase Match

3. SEO

4. Email

Email marketing is the act of sending a commercial message, typically to a group of people (potential or current customer), using email.

5. Display Ads

is advertising on websites or apps through banners or other ad formats made of text, images, flash, video, and audio.

广告位卖家 ——> 中间商display ads broker(Google Ads, social media) <– 做广告的公司(广告位买家)bidding

6. Social media

Social media marketing is the use of social media platforms and websites to promote a product or service.

7. Affiliates（返现网）

Affiliates marketing is a type of performance-based marketing in which a business rewards one or more affiliates for each visitor or customer brought by the affiliate’s own marketing efforts.

8. Comparison Shopping Engine

A comparison shopping website, sometimes called a price comparison website, Price Analysis tool, comparison shopping agent, shopbot.

Most comparison shopping sites aggregate product listings from many different retailers but do not directly sell products themselves, instead earning money from affiliate marketing agreements.

eg. 订机票/酒店的网站
